JUL-31, 2011 | Pintolência - Time to violence!

Save your egg building the correct chicken and use it to kill your enemy… o/
Use mouse to combine elements (molotov, candy and music) and create your chicken.

To combine, drag the elements up to your egg.
- molotov + music = rock’n’roll chicken
- molotov + candy = fire chicken
- candy + music = pink chicken

Using rock, paper and scissor logic:
- rock’n’roll chicken KILL fire chicken
- fire chicken KILL pink chicken
- pink chicken KILL rock’n’roll chicken

Have fun! or not… xD

TEAM
ART - Guilherme Guilb
DEV - Felipe Padilha, Gregorio Benatti
